Pirates and Gold

Rules:

The n pirates stand in a known order. The first pirate suggests a distribution of the k gold between the group of pirates. After declaring the distribution, the n pirates vote. If more than half of the pirates vote yes, the suggested distribution passes. Otherwise, that pirate is executed and we recurse to n-1 pirates, still distributing k gold. Pirates have the following preferences:

  • Pirates want to live.
  • Pirates want maximum gold.
  • Pirates like killing other pirates.
